For the first time ever, Lee Chae-min will meet his Indonesian fans through 2025 LEE CHAE MIN FANMEETING TOUR ‘Chaem-into you’ in JAKARTA, an evening filled with warmth and heartfelt moments!

Known for his captivating performances—especially in Bon Appetit, Your Majesty, as well as Crash Course in Romance, Hierarchy, See You in My 19th Life, and High Class—Lee Chae-min is ready to reveal his genuine and charming personality before his fans.

During this event, the audience will be invited to relive Lee Chae-min’s journey and personal stories through light-hearted talk sessions, interactive segments full of surprises, and a special performance exclusively prepared for this show. The warmth and intimacy of this event make it more than just a fan meeting — it’s a meaningful moment to get to know Lee Chae-min more closely.

Don’t miss this rare opportunity to share the same space with one of Korea’s most promising young actors. Seats are very limited — secure your tickets only through the official platform and be part of an unforgettable night with Lee Chae-min!

Untuk pertama kalinya, Lee Chae-min akan bertemu langsung dengan penggemar Indonesia melalui 2025 LEE CHAE IN FANMEETING TOUR ‘Chaem-into you’ in JAKARTA yang penuh kehangatan!

Dikenal lewat aktingnya yang memikat, khususnya di Bon Appetit, Your Majesty, juga di di Crash Course in Romance, Hierarchy, See You in My 19th Life, dan High Class, Lee Chae-min siap menunjukkan pesona aslinya yang tulus di hadapan para penggemar.

Dalam acara ini, penonton akan diajak menikmati perjalanan karier dan kisah pribadi Lee Chae-min lewat sesi talk show yang ringan, segmen interaktif penuh kejutan, serta special performance yang hanya bisa disaksikan di acara ini. Kehangatan dan keintiman acara ini menjadikannya lebih dari sekadar pertemuan — ini adalah momen berharga untuk mengenal sosok Lee Chae-min lebih dekat.

Jangan lewatkan kesempatan langka untuk berada di ruang yang sama dengan aktor muda yang tengah bersinar ini. Tempat duduk sangat terbatas — amankan tiketmu hanya melalui platform resmi dan jadilah bagian dari malam yang tak terlupakan bersama Lee Chae-min!

Special Needs

  1. To ensure safety and comfort during the show,
    pregnant women, wheelchair users, and individuals with special needs will be allocated a designated area.
  2. Are companions allowed?
    Companions are required to purchase their own tickets.
  3. How to register for special needs?
    Please send an email to info@threemountainsave.com with the subject “LEE CHAE MIN JAKARTA – SPECIAL NEED” no later than November 12, 2025, and include the following information:
    + Full name (as stated on your ID)
    + Order ID
    + Number of companions
    + Medical certificate or doctor’s note

Kebutuhan Khusus

  1. Untuk memastikan keamanan dan kenyamanan selama menonton pertunjukan,
    ibu hamil, pengguna kursi roda, dan penonton berkebutuhan khusus akan ditempatkan di area khusus.
  2. Apakah pendamping diperbolehkan?
    Para pendamping wajib membeli tiket tersendiri.
  3. Bagaimana cara mendaftar kebutuhan khusus?
    Wajib mengirim email ke info@threemountainsave.com dengan subjek “LEE CHAE MIN JAKARTA – SPECIAL NEED” paling lambat 12 November 2025 dan mencantumkan:
    + Nama lengkap sesuai kartu identitas
    + Order ID
    + Jumlah pendamping
    + Surat keterangan dokter
